About Me

I'm currently a second-year graduate student at Georgia Institute of Techonology (Georgia Tech / GT). I do research with Dr. John Wise on the formation of direct-collapse black holes in the early universe. I also have several years of experience with the IceCube Collaboration at Michigan State University (MSU), including using machine learning (ML) for event reconstruction and a statistical study of detecting neutrinos from dark matter (DM) particle annihilation.
